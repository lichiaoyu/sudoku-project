How to Play

Follow these simple rules to master the garden of numbers.

The Grid

A standard 9x9 Sudoku grid is made of 3x3 subgrids. Some cells are pre-filled as "fixed" numbers.

Filling Cells

Each row, column, and 3x3 box must contain all numbers from 1 to 9 without any repetition.

Win Condition

The game ends when all empty cells are filled correctly according to the logic rules.

Scoring

Your score is based on the difficulty level and the time taken to complete the puzzle.
